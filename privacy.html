Privacy
Policy

Last updated March 24, 2026. We take your privacy seriously — here is exactly how we handle your data.

Who we are

Auxia is an AI-powered virtual receptionist service founded by Emiliano and Julio Carrizales Padilla. We help real estate agents and small businesses automate their customer communication through WhatsApp and SMS.

What data we collect

When you use Auxia or interact with an Auxia-powered assistant, we may collect:

  • Contact information such as name, phone number, and email address
  • Conversation data from WhatsApp and SMS interactions
  • Appointment and booking information
  • Business information provided during onboarding and setup
  • Usage data and interaction logs

How we use your data

We use collected data to provide and improve our AI receptionist service, respond to messages and book appointments on behalf of our clients, improve our AI models using anonymized data only, send service-related communications, and comply with legal obligations.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with our clients who receive conversation logs relevant to their business, with service providers under strict confidentiality agreements, and with law enforcement only when legally required.

WhatsApp and SMS

Conversations processed through Auxia are subject to WhatsApp's and your carrier's terms of service in addition to this policy. We process messages only to deliver the service. Conversation content is stored securely and not used for advertising.

Data retention

We retain conversation data for up to 12 months unless a client requests earlier deletion. Account information is retained while the account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at [email protected].

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at [email protected]. We will respond within 30 days.

Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. Our infrastructure runs on private servers with access controls, monitoring, and regular security audits.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify active clients of material changes via email at least 14 days before they take effect.
